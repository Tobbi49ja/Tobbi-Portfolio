About Me

Who am I?

I'm a passionate Full-Stack Developer and creative Designer with 8 months of hands-on experience crafting responsive, visually engaging, and user-focused digital products. I specialize in frontend and backend development using modern technologies like HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, React, Vite, Node.js, and Express, while also excelling in UI/UX and graphic design with tools like Figma, Canva, and Corel Draw.

My development approach is rooted in clean, efficient code and thoughtful design. I believe great software isn’t just functional—it should feel intuitive and be a pleasure to use. Whether I’m building a full-stack web app or designing a pixel-perfect interface, I aim to create seamless user experiences.

Currently open to remote or on-site roles, I bring a strong foundation in software engineering, a collaborative mindset, and a passion for solving problems in innovative ways. I’ve contributed to freelance and open-source projects, led creative processes, and thrived in fast-paced, resource-constrained environments.

Beyond the screen, I unwind and recharge through my love for gaming, watching movies, and hanging out with friends. Whether I’m exploring new digital worlds, catching the latest films, or enjoying a fun outing, I believe in balancing hard work with genuine enjoyment and creativity.
